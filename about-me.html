Edward Eaton - About Me

Edward R. Eaton is a consultant in automotive fluids experienced in research, testing, development and certification. Areas of focus include engine coolant / antifreeze testing, diesel fuel and Diesel Exhaust Fluid (DEF).

ASTM D3306 and D6210 are important engine coolant testing protocols. The ISO 22241 protocol for Diesel Exhaust Fluid Testing is the companion fluid used in today's "clean diesel" engines to reduce NOx Emissions known in selective catalytic reduction technology (SCR). DEF Testing is critical to ensure the purity of the fluid, so that the DEF does not cause problems that can cause the vehicle to shut down.
