Edward Eaton - Formulation Assistance

During my long career in the automotive fluids industry, my role as an engineer has been to research, develop and commercialize the chemistry that results in value-added products. These products have a predictable life cycle, and need to be cost-optimized, improved, and replaced on a regular basis. I have been through that process dozens of times and am able to contribulte to prompt and efficient product life-cycle solutions for products related to:

  • Antifreeze / Coolant (engine coolant, HVAC fluids, various glycol bases)
  • Diesel Fuel and Diesel Fuel Additives
  • Gasoline performance additives
  • Engine Coolant Recycling evolution
  • Engine Coolant Supplemental Coolant Additives
